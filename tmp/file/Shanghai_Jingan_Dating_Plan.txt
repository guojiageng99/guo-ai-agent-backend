SHANGHAI JING'AN DISTRICT ROMANTIC DATING PLAN
================================================

For your partner in Jing'an District, Shanghai!

THEME: "Autumn Strolls & Artful Moments"
Recommended for autumn evenings (October-November) when Yuyuan Road's plane trees are golden.

STARTING POINT: Jing'an Temple Metro Station (Lines 2/14)

ITINERARY:

1. 4:30-5:30 PM: Yuyuan Road Stroll
   • Walk hand-in-hand under iconic plane trees with falling autumn leaves
   • Stop at charming cafes like 'Café de Flore' or 'The Nest'
   • Capture romantic photos against historic architecture

2. 5:30-6:30 PM: Jing'an Temple Visit
   • Visit this 1,200-year-old Buddhist temple, beautifully lit in the evening
   • Light incense together for good fortune in your relationship
   • Enjoy the peaceful garden and traditional architecture

3. 6:30-7:30 PM: Dinner at Qinghe Valley
   • Address: 555 An Yuan Road, Jing'an District
   • Authentic Korean cuisine in romantic setting with outdoor seating
   • Hours: 5:00-11:00 PM

4. 7:30-9:00 PM: Fotografiska Art Center
   • Asia's flagship photography museum in Suzhou Creek area
   • Explore world-class exhibitions
   • Enjoy cocktails at rooftop bar with city views

5. 9:00-9:30 PM: Jing'an Park Night Walk
   • Peaceful stroll through beautifully lit park
   • Find quiet bench to enjoy night air and reflect on your time together

TRANSPORTATION TIPS:
• All locations within easy walking distance (5-10 minutes)
• Jing'an Temple station is central - use exits 1 or 2
• Consider shared bikes for extra fun

PHOTO OPPORTUNITIES:
• Yuyuan Road's golden plane tree canopy
• Jing'an Temple's illuminated main hall at dusk
• Fotografiska's modern architecture reflected in Suzhou Creek
• Qinghe Valley's cozy outdoor seating area
• Jing'an Park's tranquil lake at night

PRO TIPS:
• Book Qinghe Valley in advance for best table
• Check Fotografiska's current exhibition schedule online
• Bring light jacket - evenings can be cool by the creek
• Download Didi app for easy taxi rides

This plan balances romance, culture, and Shanghai's unique charm - creating memories that will last long after the evening ends! 🌹
