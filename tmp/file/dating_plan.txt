ROMANTIC DATE PLAN FOR JING'AN DISTRICT, SHANGHAI

TOP 5 ROMANTIC LOCATIONS WITHIN 5KM:

1. JING'AN PARK
- Location: Opposite Jing'an Temple, Nanjing West Road
- Features: Free 24-hour access, outdoor cafes, lotus ponds, walking paths
- Best for: Morning strolls or evening walks
- Transport: Metro lines 2/7/14 - Jing'an Temple Station (3-min walk)

2. HALATION BISTRO/LOUNGE (Light Halo Restaurant)
- Location: 800 Changde Road, 800 Show Park
- Features: Rose garden, starry ceiling, romantic ambiance
- Hours: 10:00-24:00

3. CAFE LIANG AT JING'AN SHANGRI-LA
- Location: 1218 Yan'an Middle Road
- Features: Elegant decor, candlelight, premium cuisine

4. MONEY SHOPS AT JING'AN JIU GUANG
- Location: Jing'an Jiu Guang Department Store
- Features: Beautiful window seating, sophisticated atmosphere

5. BUTTERFLY BAY PARK & JING'AN CHILDREN'S LIBRARY
- Location: Kangding East Road 28, Suzhou River
- Features: Riverside views, historic architecture

RECOMMENDED ITINERARY:

Classic Romantic Day (6 hours):
- 10:00-12:00: Jing'an Park morning stroll & coffee
- 12:00-13:30: Lunch at nearby café
- 13:30-15:30: Jing'an Temple area exploration
- 15:30-17:00: Butterfly Bay Park relaxation
- 18:30-21:00: Dinner at Halation Bistro/Lounge

Evening Romance (3 hours):
- 18:00-19:00: Sunset walk in Jing'an Park
- 19:00-22:00: Dinner at Cafe Liang
- 22:00-23:00: Night stroll along Nanjing West Road

TRANSPORTATION TIPS:
- All locations accessible by Metro (lines 2, 7, 14)
- Jing'an Temple Station is central hub
- Most locations within 5-10 minutes walk of each other

ROMANTIC TIPS:
- Bring flowers or small gift
- Book restaurant reservations in advance
- Check weather forecast
- Capture memories with photos
- End with walk under city lights
